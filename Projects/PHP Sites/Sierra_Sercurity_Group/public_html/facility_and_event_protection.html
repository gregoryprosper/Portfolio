The Sierra Facilities/Event Management Team

At Sierra Security, there isn't an event too small or a facility too large. Our project managers and officers possess extensive experience in planning and providing short-term and long-term security services for small to mid-size events like: concerts, festivals, parties, etc as well as facilities like: hospitals, hotels, malls, neighborhoods, car dealerships, storefronts, etc. Our management team will collaborate with you to develop a highly affordable security scheme that molds to your needs. Our services included the provision of armed & unarmed security officers as well as professionally installed state-of-the-art security camera and entry control systems.

Sierra Officers (agents, guards)

Experienced - ​​We understand your need to mitigate risk while facilitating a safe and serene atmosphere for your customers. Our event and facility security officers are trained to display an authoritative, non-aggressive posture unless additional intervention is absolutely necessary. Our officers are trained and certified in the latest non-violent crisis intervention techniques (NVCI) to subdue anyone who is posing a serious threat to others in the safest way possible. All Sierra officers are trained first responders and certified in C.P.R and the use of an A.E.D. Nearly all of our officers have elite military or law enforcement experience and have been proven in the most stressful situations imaginable.
